Dylan Youmans

Hello! I'm Dylan Youmans, a 22-year-old aspiring UX/UI Designer currently completing my senior year at Georgia Southern University, majoring in Computer Science. I've dedicated the past 7 years to self-taught graphic design, relying on Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator for my creative endeavors. In the realm of web and UX/UI design, I've been crafting my skills for about 2 years, utilizing Figma and Visual Studio Code as my primary tools. Beyond design, I'm a proficient programmer, well-versed in languages such as Java, Python, and JavaScript. This portfolio serves as a showcase of my diverse skill set, encompassing graphic design, web development, UX/UI design, and programming.
